Description

August is an ordinary boy who loves video games, walking with his dog, watching "Star Wars", fighting with his sister and sorrying. But at the same time he is not an ordinary boy, he never went to school. He was born with a genetic deformity - He has no face. He conducted 30 surgeries and yet still does not look like other children. One day he will have to go to school. His peers treat him badly, but he is determined to prove that appearance does not matter.
