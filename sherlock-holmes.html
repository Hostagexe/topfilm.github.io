Description

When the serial killer, occultist "magician" Lord Blackwood is finally caught, the legendary investigator Sherlock Holmes and his assistant, Dr. Watson, are given a chance to complete another case successfully. But when Blackwood miraculously recovers from the grave and continues to have fun with the murders, Holmes starts hunting for him again. Confronted with his partner’s fiancée and Scottland Yard’s stupid boss, the fearless detective has to open a case that leads to a murder murder network, betrayal, and black magic. At the same time, he must withstand the tempting onslaught of Irene Adler.
